BeeCanvas

BeeCanvas is a visual collobartion tool based on the functions of interactive whiteboard. BeeCanvas offers you a unified visual workspace for your project. It’s the easiest way to express your thoughts, share your ideas, and communicate with your team.

Collect everything in one place

Files, Images, Links, Youtube, SoundClound.
Collect and organize everything visually in one place.
BeeCanvas is a visual collaboration tool that lets you share and comment a image, links, or other multimedia resources on a canvas whatever you want.

Designed for visual projects

BeeCanvas serves as a whiteboard for graphic and visually driven projects, with a ‘refrigerator’ to store important elements or items. It’s like Google docs for designers, artists, and those working on visual projects.
