Undestanding

TownUs New Version, Group buying platform for all organizations

The Town Company has started the TownUs 2.0 project to become a group purchase commerce platform for all groups as well as college students. There was a lot of planning to change to reflect business strategy changes in service. Among them, new UX improvements in the purchase room opening and buying process were needed.

Opportunity

Complex Group Buying, How to make it easier?

It is simple to purchase products from general commerce. You can select a product and purchase it. However, purchases in TownUs is not possible without going through a 'purchase room'.

* What is a purchase room? We have planned a 'purchase oom' (a tool room) to create a virtual space for customers to collect their products at the lowest possible price.

  • There are therefore two ways in which a customer can purchase a product.

    • 01 : Launch a purchase room. > Host
    • 02 : Participate in the already launched purchase room. > Guest

The goal of this project was to create a platform where all purchase room host and participants could easily join the group purchase.

Research and problem

Analysis of existing group purchase of TownUs

Priority has been given to finding out what kind of group purchasing are made in existing platform, what kinds of products are being sold, and what kind of problems in the buying process. After intensive research, we found three problems.

  • How to make the purchase room?

    The process of creating a purchase room was complex and confusing. First of all, it was important to make the customer to understand what the purchase room was.

  • The concept of Town

    TownUS has a core function that binds a user named 'Town', which is a upper concept of ‘purchase room’, but it has not been delivered well to the users on the existing platform.

  • Complex group buying process

    The existing group purchasing process is complicated and the steps are subdivided. The CS team is inefficient system that manages the customer by hand.

WIREFRAME - THREE IDEAS

IDEA 01 - Encourage open the purchase room

In order to make the purchase of TownUs active, we needed to make opening the purchase room should be easy and reachable. If you do not participate in the purchase room, you will not be able to buy the products. So I needed to to create a virtuous cycle in which we had to have a lot of purchase room to give more choices for users to participate.

• A purchase room opening button of the same size as other purchase rooms on the main screen -> To encourage users to open the purchase room and see products
• Floating buttons at all times also make it reachable to open the room

WIREFRAME - THREE IDEAS

IDEA 02 - Open the purchase room

For the better management of the group buying in town, the third tab ‘town’ has list of user’s town. When the user enter the town, there is the list of the purchase room in town , order history and people who are joined in the town. It is made for continuous group purchase within the ‘town’ rather than a temporary one-time purchase in a purchasing room.

• In the first stage of the purchase room opening, I proposed to put a walkthrough of 3 or 4 cuts into an illustration or picture of what the ‘Purchase room' is.

• If you click 'Select Town', you can select your town. Through this step, the user naturally can learn that there is the upper concept of the town.

WIREFRAME - THREE IDEAS

IDEA 03 - Concept of the purchase room

One of the most important improvement goals of the TownUs 2.0 project was the concept of a ‘purchase room'. I carefully arranged and designed all the UI elements, since customers can purchase products in the room and they had to aware of the buying process well enough without any help of CS team.

• It was important to intuitively communicate the current state of the purchase of in the room.
• Added interactions to move to the next level, such as starting and closing a group purchase.
• Designed to be friendly to the users who entered the first room by exposing the town, and host of the purchase room at the top.

Final design - Purchase Room

Open the purchase room

There are two steps to open a purchase room.
1) Select the town 2) Enter purchase room information

It was a boring but unavoidable step to open the purchase room, and in the first step I tried to provide an intimate experience by providing an illustration of the purchase room. Info buttons were placed throughout to help understand the purchase process.
