Seoul Logo Design

The goal of the 'Seoul Logo Design' project was to create a new logo of Seoul. There is no specific logo to represent Seoul which is loved and acknowledged by people. I have thought about the reason why Seoul does not have so powerful logo.

I concluded that it could not gain a certain sympathy to move people. I think a logo has to attract attention through arousing sympathy. My focus was especially targeted to make people imagine Seoul to be a city full of fun and having many entertainments. After focusing on this concept i was able to come up with 3 logos to represent Seoul.

Tasty Seoul

The first logo is 'Tasty Seoul'. Since Seoul is a perfect city to try out the delicious oriental Korean cuisine, I could not hlep but focus on the dining aspect. I used 'Futura' font and made 'e' resemble an eye, which makes this logo have a definite feature of a ludicrous experssion. The purpose was to relate a picture of someone eating something tasty. When the word 'tasty' is omitted in the logo, it is also possible to deliver an impression of Seoul as an enjoyable city. I believe this logo has several interpretations and therefore can be used in various situations.

Romantic Seoul

I focused on making an image of Seoul to be a city full of romance and warm-hearted people. This logo does not only represent love between people, but also the human bond and connection that exist between friends and family.

Simply Seoul

N tower is the perfect place to experience the amazing view of Seoul. There is no doubt about the fact that N tower is the most powerful landmark of Seoul. This is the reason why I chose the caption 'simply' to represent Seoul.
