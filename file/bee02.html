  • OVERVIEW

    BeeCanvas is a visual collobartion tool based on the functions of interactive whiteboard. BeeCanvas offers a unified visual workspace for creative projects.

    Working as a product designer, I participated in making contents marketing as well. I wrote articles and made commercials for promoting BeeCanvas. Closely communicating with our clients and identifying their needs was helpful to make marketing stratgy.

Challenges

WHY BEECANVAS

BeeCanvas is a visual collaboration tool based on functions interactive whiteboard. It was important to explain when and why people have difficulty doing creative work and convince people to use BeeCanvas.

Step 01 : User research

Interviews with potential customers

To undestand how creative people work and what kind of productivity tool they use in their fields was essential. Through in-depth interviews with creative people in various fields, for example, brand designer, fashion designer, project manager, architecture, writer, I found a common difficulty that they have when sharing their rough ideas and organizing various types of media in one place.
"key finiding"

Step 02 : Competitive research

The strength of BeeCanvas compared with Evernote

After identifying their needs for visually organizing stuff, I came up with an idea that it would be effective to explain BeeCanvas by comparing with Evernote. Evernote was the most well-known productivity app which offers the opposite UX of BeeCanvas. I wrote an article about why I began using BeeCanvas as a user not a designer instead of Evernote which I have been using for several years.

I showed an example of my Evernote which was used for ideations about city branding commercial with my teammates. We gathered various types of media, like youtube videos, web links, pdf files, music files. It was not easily to make connections between things and organize this information at a glance on Evernote.

User Scenerio

Visually organize ideas with BeeCanvas

I illustrated the user scenario of what..... Problems that I had on Evernote were solved on BeeCanvas because it was much easier to gather and make connections between all kinds of media together and make comments on the certain objects.

I explained BeeCanvas is useful in terms of creative thinking process. Creative thinking expands in multiple directions, rather than in one direction. The painpoint of Evernote is that users can only type text choronologically and linearlly, which makes you less creative.

I pinpoint that on BeeCanvas, users can gain efficiency and effectiveness by making the process visible on the canvas, which is impossible on Evernote. BeeCanvas helps others to 'get inside our heads' in order to convey a thematic setting for a design or to explain function in piece of work.

Offering benefits & Usecase

Workspace for visual projects

Based on the user research, I showed actual use cases of BeeCanvas to make potential customers see how they can adapt BeeCanvas to their working process.

Explaining why using Evernote is less you creative was not enough and we needed to show how creative ideas evolve down different paths on BeeCanvas.

Outcome

We saw positive results— 30,000 views and 1,000 shares of the article via SNS and Facebook! Comparison with Evernote was effective to make people understand BeeCanvas is for visual creative projects. People got familiar with BeeCanvas as the opposite productivity tool of Evernote.

Beyond the static image

Making of tutorial video

For better understaning of how to use BeeCanvas, I made several basic tutorials of BeeCanvas. For users who firstly use BeeCanvas, I focused on basic guides to use toolbar.

Landing page design & Copywriting

An Eye-catching Tagline

I suggested a tagline of BeeCanvas based on the contents of article that i wrote. It is ”We know your ideas are more than just words.” The tagline was effective to draw sympathy of users and the mockup image was also well matched with the tagline.
