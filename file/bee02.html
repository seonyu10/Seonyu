Problem

WHY BEECANVAS

BeeCanvas is a visual collaboration tool based on functions interactive whiteboard. It was important to explain when and why people have difficulty doing creative work and convince people to use BeeCanvas. Not only explaining BeeCanvas is place for gathering all formats of files in one place, providing proper usecases to different types of creative people was important.

User research

Interviews with potential customers

To undestand how actually creative people work and what kind of productivity tool they use in their fields was extremly important. Through deep interviews with creative people in various fields, I found some common difficulty when sharing their rough ideas with people and organizing various types of file. I organized all the interviews with potential users on BeeCanvas.

Approach

The limit of Evernote

To explain easily how BeeCanvas is useful, I needed to compare BeeCanvas with the other services. Evernote was the most well-known note-taking app which offers the opposite UX of BeeCanvas. I wrote an article about why I began using BeeCanvas instead of Evernote which I have been using for a long time.

I showed an example of my Evernote in the article. The Evernote was a reference research documents shared with my teammates, which was used for documenting about city branding commercial. We gathered various types of medias, like youtube videos, web links, pdf files, music file and so on. It was not easily to see and organize all types of media at a glance on Evernote because it is basically note-taking app which is impossible to visually display all the information that I gathered.

Solution

Visually organize ideas with BeeCanvas

I explained why BeeCanvas is useful in term of creative thinking process. Human thought characterized by expansion in multiple directions, rather than in one direction, and based on the concept that there are multiple starting points which from many types of information. On Evernote, users think linearly, which is a process of thought following known step-by-step progression. But on BeeCanvas, it was much easier to make comments on certain objects, which became much more fun when collaborating with teammates. BeeCanvas let us express our ideas more freely and visually on the canvas.

Offering benefits & Usecase

Designed for visual projects

I showed several use cases of BeeCanvas to make potential users understand easily. I draw sympathy that people get inpirations from many types of media, which can be text, pictures, videos, links or music. I pinpoint that there is a moment that people want to bring all the sources and organize them visually without any limits. For those needs, I suggested that BeeCanvas is the most suitable tool.
