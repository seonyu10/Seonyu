Problem

WHY BEECANVAS

BeeCanvas is a visual collaboration tool based on functions interactive whiteboard. It was important to show when and why people have difficulty doing creative work and convince people to use BeeCanvas. To explain easily how effective it is to visualize ideas, I needed to compare BeeCanvas with the other services. Evernote was the most well-known note-taking app which offers the opposite UX of BeeCanvas. I wrote a blog about why I began using BeeCanvas instead of Evernote which I have been using for a long time.

APPROACH

The restrictions of Evernote

To explain easily, I showed an example of my Evernote. The Evernote was a reference research documents shared with my teammates, which was for the competition of city branding commercial. For making a commericial, we collected various types of medias, like youtube videos, web links, pdf files, music file and so on. It was not easily to see and organize all types of media at a glance on Evernote because it is basically note-taking app which is impossible to visually display all the information that I gathered.

Designed for visual projects

BeeCanvas serves as a whiteboard for graphic and visually driven projects.It’s like Google docs for designers, artists, and those working on visual projects. I did contents marketing for promoting BeeCanvas : Writing articles and making commercials.
